Welcome!

You may have noticed that the data in Reflect and Flashers gets erased when you clear your browser's data. This occurs because the data is temporarily stored in the browser’s localStorage. However, there’s a solution: you can save your data by using the Download option in either Reflect or Flashers. Later, you can restore the data by copying and pasting the downloaded file into the Import page of the respective tool.
