Announcements

Welcome! You might have noticed that the data in Reflect and Flashers gets cleared when you clear the browser's data. This happens because these data are stored temporarily in the broswer's 'localstorage'. However, I have made a way out of this problem: you can save the data (of Reflect or Flashers) by clicking the download option in Reflect or Flashers, then you can restore the data by copy-pasting the data (that was downloaded if you followed the previous steps correctly) in the import page of Reflect or Flashers.

If you encounter a bug, please report it to me as it would help me to improve the apps. On a side note, you are welcome to suggest me about any new sum in CosmicMath. Thank you!
